Responsive Spacing, Grid & Container

One spacing scale (4→128px), one container max-width (1360px), gutters that step down at tablet/mobile breakpoints.

Border, Shadow, Glow & Glass System

Hairline borders that brighten (never thicken) on hover/active. Soft, large-radius ambient shadows. Colored glow reserved for focus/importance. Glass reserved for nav, overlays, and modals only — never on content cards.

glass-surface (nav/overlay/modal only)

Button Variants

Exactly one gold CTA per view. Outline/ghost for secondary actions. Fill-from-edge hover on gold, never an instant color snap.

Card Variants

Biography Card and Category Card share one anatomy: image fills frame, bottom scrim for legibility, chip top-left, label bottom-left, arrow affordance bottom-right on navigable cards.
